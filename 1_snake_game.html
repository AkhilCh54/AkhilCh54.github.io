Snake Game (OOP)

The Snake game, made famous by Nokia in 1997, became a worldwide sensation and remains a nostalgic favorite to this day.

I built a version of it using Python. Here’s a quick breakdown of how it works:

  • Developed using object-oriented programming (OOP) with four components: main.py, snake.py, scoreboard.py, and food.py.
  • main.py coordinates the game logic and ties everything together.
  • snake.py creates and manages the movement of the snake.
  • food.py randomly generates food on the screen, and replaces it once eaten.
  • scoreboard.py handles and displays the player's score.
